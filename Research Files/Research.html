Our team conducts exhaustive research of the economy and bilateral trade relations between India and Canada. We gather data for trade in all sectors and other important parameters such as Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) to generate statistics and present data models in a visually appealing way using analytics and APIs. Our research directors work closely with the technical directors to deliver these data models. 

The following models have been implemented which will cater to the needs of various government agencies, private companies and educational institutions.
